Types of Bikes

Mountain Bikes

Built for off-road adventures. Features durable frames, wide tires, and suspension systems. Ideal for tackling rugged trails and rocky terrain.

Rental Rate: $20/day

Road Bikes

Designed for speed and efficiency on paved roads. Lightweight frames and narrow tires minimize rolling resistance. Perfect for long-distance rides and competitive racing.

Rental Rate: $25/day

Hybrid Bikes

Combines features of road and mountain bikes. Offers versatility for both city commuting and light off-road trails. Provides a comfortable upright riding position.

Rental Rate: $30/day

Cruiser Bikes

Known for their classic, retro style. Designed for leisurely rides on flat terrain. Features wide, cushioned seats and swept-back handlebars for a relaxed riding experience.

Rental Rate: $15/day

Electric Bikes

Equipped with an electric motor to assist pedaling. Offers varying levels of assistance, making cycling easier. Ideal for commuting, especially over hilly terrain, and for riders seeking an extra boost.

Rental Rate: $35/day
